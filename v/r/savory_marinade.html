savory marinade

the name is generic because it's so versatile! it's delicious on tofu and vegetables, especially brussels sprouts.

ingredients

  • c/4 oil (sunflower or canola is fine)
  • c/4 soy sauce or tamari
  • T garlic powder
  • T dried thyme

directions

you just mix it in a bowl! then cover whatever you're making. for brussels sprouts i recommend slicing them in half, so the marinade can soak inside.
