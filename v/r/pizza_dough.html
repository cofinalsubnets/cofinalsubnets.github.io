2/3 pizza dough

this recipe uses 2/3 as much water as flour by weight. i often add some extra seasoning to the dry mix, like garlic powder or oregano. if you're in a hurry you can double the yeast.

ingredients

  • 600g flour (~25c/6)
  • 16g salt
  • 8g dry yeast
  • 16g olive oil (~5T/4)
  • 400g water (400ml or 5c/3)

directions

mix dry ingredients in a bowl. add water and oil and mix with hands until all the flour is incorporated. knead on floured surface until smooth. return to bowl, cover, and let rise 45-60m.
