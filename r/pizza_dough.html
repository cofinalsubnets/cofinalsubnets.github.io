60% pizza dough

this recipe uses 60% hydration, hence the name. i often add some extra seasoning to the dry mix, like garlic powder or oregano. if you're in a hurry you can double the yeast.

ingredients

  • 300g flour
  • 8g salt
  • 4g dry yeast
  • 200g water
  • 8g olive oil

directions

mix dry ingredients in a bowl. add water and oil and mix with hands until all the flour is incorporated. turn out onto floured surface and knead until smooth. return to bowl, cover, and let rise 45-60m.
