veg chili

You can make this in one pot. Obviously you can substitute other beans or veggies. For extra protein & meaty texture, add TVP along with the beans & tomatoes. For extra thickness add masa. This chili has a little bit of heat; if you don't want that then use half as much chili powder & omit the cayenne.

ingredients

first veggies

  • T oil
  • c onion, diced
  • c celery, diced
  • c carrot, diced
  • c bell pepper, diced

second veggies

  • c/2 cut green beans
  • c/2 corn
  • T garlic, minced

spices

  • 3T/2 chili powder
  • T/2 cumin
  • T salt (or to taste)
  • T oregano
  • T smoked paprika (or reg paprika, or chipotle powder)
  • t black pepper
  • t cayenne powder

wet stuff

  • ~30oz can crushed tomato
  • ~15oz can chickpeas, with liquid, ideally pulsed in chopper / food processor
  • ~15oz can red kidney beans, with liquid
  • ~15oz can black beans, with liquid
  • TVP to desired meatiness (optional)
  • masa to desired thickness (optional)
  • extra water as needed

directions

heat oil & saute first veggies until carrots are cooked. add second veggies & spices, cook a few minutes more. add wet stuff, mix, reduce heat & cover. simmer 20-30m, stirring occasionally.
