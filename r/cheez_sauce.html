cheez sauce

this is the best vegan cheez sauce recipe i've found! it's perfect for mac and cheez, but it's also thick enough that you can use it as a condiment or spread. i usually make it with cornstarch, because it's easier to find and less expensive than arrowroot. if you have arrowroot starch, it will give a stretchier, more cheeselike texture.

ingredients

  • 3c/2 coconut milk (the unsweetened kind from a can)
  • 6T nutritional yeast
  • 4T starch (cornstarch is fine, but ideally arrowroot or tapioca)
  • t salt
  • t onion powder
  • t/2 garlic powder
  • t/4 smoked paprika

directions

mix everything in a saucepan and stir constantly over medium heat until thickened. it'll firm up as it cools, but will relax if you mix it into (hot) cooked pasta.
