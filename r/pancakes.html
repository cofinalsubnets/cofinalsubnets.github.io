pancakes

yep, they're just pancakes. using c/4 mashed pumpkin for the egglike makes them orange and extra fun.

ingredients

dry

  • c flour
  • 2T sugar
  • 2t baking powder
  • pinch salt

wet

  • one egglike
  • c milklike
  • 2T oil

directions

mix dry ingredients in a bowl, and wet ingredients in another bowl. add wet to dry and whisk until just combined and still clumpy. ladle into a pan and cook on medium-high.
